What Sets Wrap Media Apart

Unlike other great technology companies started by first time founders, Wrap Media’s founding team of leadership has numerous wins building and leading great technology companies. We are channeling this experience to build something remarkable at Wrap Media.

  • CEO/ Founder of Wrap Media founded & took public companies Viant and Scient.
  • Closed Series B for a total of $16 million within a year of launching the company, led by Raine Ventures and Salesforce Ventures.
  • Wrap Media is the first “WE” corporation, 10% of Wrap’s capitalization is willed to philanthropy.
  • As a WE corporation, we hire leaders, visionaries and advocates of a meaningful work culture that inspires people to challenge themselves and use their voice.
  • Leadership team comes from Yahoo, Salesforce.com, Oracle, Frog Design, Ebay, Samsung, PayPal, Bazaarvoice, Icix, Terracotta, Pantheon, InMobi, Scribd and AudioPress.

life at Wrap Media

Wrap Media attracts the thoughtful, naturally inquisitive and spirited. We think about mobile from new perspectives. We encourage each other to push boundaries. Our office is based in downtown San Francisco convenient to all public transportation. Our people enjoy access to the best tools available, an open and collaborative work environment and a supportive culture inspiring them to do their very best. We offer great salaries, generous equity,100% employee health coverage, three weeks vacation and delicious catered meals among other perks. We encourage you to learn more about working here!
